Anti-Fraud Requirements

No Fake Hours: You must only report actual hours worked on this project. Hours are verified through Hackatime tracking and manipulation will result in immediate account suspension.
No Double Submissions: You cannot submit the same project for multiple YSWS programs, contests, or grants. Each submission must be unique and original.
Own Work Only: You must only submit projects you personally created. Submitting someone else's work or claiming credit for work you didn't do is strictly prohibited.
Real Information: All personal details (name, email, address, etc.) must be accurate and truthful. False information will result in automatic disqualification.
Consequences: Violation of any of these rules will result in immediate account suspension, disqualification from all programs, and potential permanent ban from Hack Club services.
